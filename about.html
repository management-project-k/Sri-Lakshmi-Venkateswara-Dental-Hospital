Our Story & Values

Lead Dentist

Dr. Rajasekhar Yadav

Dr. Rajasekhar Yadav leads Sri Lakshmi Venkateswara Dental Hospital with a patient-first philosophy. Known for his calm demeanor and clear explanations, he ensures every patient understands their treatment plan before proceeding.

With expertise spanning root canal therapy, zirconia restorations, dental implants, laser dentistry, and cosmetic procedures, Dr. Yadav combines clinical precision with genuine care.

His approach is simple: listen carefully, explain clearly, treat gently, and deliver results that last.

Our Approach

What We Stand For

Every decision we make is guided by these core principles.

🧼

Hygiene First

We follow rigorous sterilization and infection-control protocols. Every instrument is autoclaved, every surface disinfected. Your safety is non-negotiable.

💆

Patient Comfort

We understand dental anxiety. Our approach prioritizes gentle handling, effective anaesthesia, and a calm environment so you feel at ease at every step.

💬

Clear Explanation

No medical jargon, no rushed consultations. Dr. Yadav takes the time to explain your condition, options, and expected outcomes in plain language.

⚖️

Ethical Practice

We recommend only what's needed — no unnecessary procedures, no inflated billing. Honest diagnostics and transparent pricing are our standard.

🎯

Precision & Skill

From intricate root canal work to detailed zirconia restorations, we bring technical excellence to every procedure we perform.

🤝

Lasting Relationships

Many of our patients have been with us for years. We build trust through consistent care, follow-ups, and genuine concern for your well-being.
