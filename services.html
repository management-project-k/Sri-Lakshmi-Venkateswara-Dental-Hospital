Dental Bonding

Repair chips, cracks, and discoloration

Dental bonding uses a tooth-coloured composite resin to repair minor imperfections — chips, cracks, gaps, or discoloration. It's a quick, affordable way to enhance your smile without extensive treatment.

Ideal for: Chipped front teeth, small gaps, stained or worn teeth.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed in the jawbone, acting as artificial roots for replacement teeth. They look, feel, and function like natural teeth — a long-lasting solution for missing teeth.

Ideal for: Single or multiple missing teeth, patients seeking a permanent alternative to dentures.

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Save your natural tooth painlessly

Root canal therapy removes infected pulp from inside a tooth, cleans and shapes the canal, then seals it to prevent further infection. Modern RCT is virtually painless with proper anaesthesia and is far preferable to losing a tooth.

Ideal for: Severe toothache, infected or abscessed teeth, deep cavities reaching the nerve.

1 X-Ray & Diagnosis
2 Pulp Removal
3 Canal Shaping
